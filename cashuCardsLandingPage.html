The honest reason this exists

Bitcoin Doesn't Need More "Bitcoin Enthusiests". It doesn't need Astrlogy charts.
It Needs More People Stacking Sats.

Bitcoin has a people problem. Not a technical problem. The technical problem was solved in 2009. The people problem is this: most humans on earth still think "bitcoin" is a corporation run by a some dude wearing a suit and drooling over red and green candles.

People who listen to 40 hours of bitcon podcasts, run their own nodes, and know how to set up a Cold Card don't need Cashu. If you know what the acronym UTXO stands for, you don't need Cashu or Fedimint. Everybody else doesn't need hard core bitcoiners like us to preach about self-custody.

Cashu is an easy way to teach people how to use bitcoin the way it was meant to be, as p2p e cash. The people who watch candles instead of holding key need Cashu Cards. The people who trust a memecoiner because he was on TV need Cashu. The people waiting for CBDC Clarity need Cashu. The people who would absolutely hold real bitcoin if someone they loved just stuffed it in an envelope and handed it to them in a birthday card need Cashu.

"Your wife hates when you talk about bitcoin, but she'll love this card. Just kidding, but she might think it's cool like Calle!'"

Your grandparents gave you cards with cash inside. You understood immediately. A physical card with a QR code redeemable for sats inside is the same gesture 2.0. You don't have to explain peer-to-peer electronic cash. You just hand them a birthday card.

Bitcoin adoption doesn't happen because you listen to 40 hours of bitoin podcasts every week. It happens through people using bitoin in the real world.. Cashu Cards allow you to trasnfer bitcoin in the real world, one card at a time.
