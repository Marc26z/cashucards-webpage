The goal of Cashu Cards is to create tangible greeting cards capable of tranfering sats to someone offline. They are novel, but the goal is to use the as a tool to teach people how to use bitxoin as electronic cash. This is the Landing Page to sell Cashu Cards made with sats, sweat, and paper.


## Why Can't I Just Make My Own Cards?
Cashu Cards are written under a permissive licence. To be honest, the author always thought greeting cards were silly. Why pay for greeting cards when anyone with a sharpie and some card stock can make them? I asked Great Ghee why should I buy Ghee when I know how to make Ghee? He said, why do people go to restaurants when they know how to cook? I had no argument.

I also like how Bitcoin was written using the MIT licence so decided to release Cashu Cards under the same licence. Want to make your own damn greeting cards? Go for it. I vibed sofme software that lets you do it yourself. (Paper, cashu and ink not included)
